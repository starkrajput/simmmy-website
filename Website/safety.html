🛡️ Your Safety Matters

Stay Safe on Simmmy

We want every connection you make to feel secure and genuine. Read our safety guidelines before you start matching.

⚠️ Never send money or financial information

Anyone who asks for money, gift cards, cryptocurrency, or your banking details is likely a scammer. This is the most common romance scam. Report them immediately through the app and contact your bank if you've already sent anything.

💬
Before You Meet
  • Chat within the Simmmy app before sharing personal contact details
  • Video call before meeting in person — it confirms they're real
  • Never share your home address, workplace, or financial information
  • Be cautious if someone asks to move off Simmmy immediately
  • Use a Google Voice or similar number if you want to share a number early
📍
Meeting in Person
  • Always meet in a public place for the first several dates
  • Tell a friend or family member where you're going and who you're meeting
  • Arrange your own transport to and from the date
  • Don't leave your drink unattended or accept drinks from strangers
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, leave
🚩
Red Flags to Watch For
  • They refuse to video call or always have excuses
  • Their profile photos look like stock images or model shots
  • They profess strong feelings very quickly ("love bombing")
  • They ask for money for any reason — medical, travel, emergency
  • Their story has inconsistencies or changes frequently
  • They pressure you to share explicit photos
🛡️
How Simmmy Protects You
  • AI photo verification on every profile
  • 24/7 AI moderation of messages for harmful patterns
  • End-to-end encryption on all messages
  • One-tap block and report on every profile and message
  • Optional ID verification for extra trust badges
  • Precise location is never shared with other users
🆘
Emergency Situations
  • If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number (112 in India/EU, 999 in UK, 911 in USA)
  • If you've been harmed, report to the police first, then contact us
  • Report incidents to safety@simmmy.com with as much detail as possible
  • We work with law enforcement and will provide data when legally required
📱
How to Report
  • In the app: Tap ⋯ on any profile or message → Report
  • Block: Tap ⋯ → Block — they won't see your profile
  • Email: safety@simmmy.com with screenshots if possible
  • All reports are reviewed by a real human within 24 hours
  • We take action on every verified report, including permanent bans
🆘

See something wrong?

Report any profile, message, or behaviour that makes you uncomfortable. Your report helps protect our entire community.
