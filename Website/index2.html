Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How does simmmy's cosmic matching actually work? ▼
simmmy's superintelligent engine analyses 47 compatibility dimensions. It combines your full natal birth chart (sun, moon, rising, Venus, Mars placements), tarot archetype, numerology life path, psychological profile (attachment style, love language, Big Five personality), lifestyle preferences, and real-time planetary transits to calculate a composite compatibility score. Unlike simple swipe apps, we look at who you are at a soul level, not just surface attraction.
Do I need to believe in astrology to use simmmy? ▼
Not at all! Many of our most successful couples were sceptics. The cosmic features are one layer of our matching — we also use rigorous psychological compatibility science. Think of astrology and tarot as a beautiful framework for self-reflection and conversation starters. Even users who don't "believe" report feeling more understood through these frameworks.
Is simmmy free to use? ▼
Yes — simmmy is free to download, create a profile, and receive matches. Our free tier includes daily curated matches, messaging, and basic compatibility scores. simmmy Premium unlocks unlimited matches, full cosmic compatibility reports, daily tarot readings, moon phase guidance, priority in searches, and access to exclusive events. Premium starts from £7.99/month.
How does simmmy keep me safe? ▼
Safety is our absolute priority. Every profile is photo-verified. Our AI moderation system monitors messages 24/7 for harmful behaviour. You can block or report any user instantly. We offer optional ID verification for an added layer of trust. Your location data is never shared with other users without your explicit consent. Read our full Safety Guidelines for more details.
Can I use simmmy to find friends, not just romance? ▼
Absolutely. simmmy's Friend Mode matches you with people for platonic connections — best friends, travel companions, business collaborators, creative partners, and more. The same cosmic compatibility engine applies. Soul connections come in all forms.
How is my data used and is it private? ▼
Your privacy is paramount. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your birth chart, psychological profile, and personal information are used solely to improve your matches and experience on simmmy. You can delete all your data at any time. Please read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
