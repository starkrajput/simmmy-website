4. ✨ Cosmic & Astrology Data

simmmy's core matching engine uses your birth date, time, and place of birth to generate a natal astrological chart, tarot archetype, and numerology life path number. This is central to what makes simmmy unique.

Important: Your exact birth time and birthplace are used only internally for compatibility calculation. They are never displayed publicly on your profile or shared with other users.

What We Do with Cosmic Data

  • Generate your natal chart, including sun, moon, rising, Venus, and Mars placements
  • Calculate your tarot archetype based on birth numerology
  • Compute cross-compatibility scores with potential matches
  • Provide daily astrological transit guidance personalised to your chart
  • Improve our cosmic matching algorithms using aggregated, anonymised astrological data

Sensitive Categories

In certain jurisdictions, birth data combined with health or religious information may be considered sensitive. Where this applies, we process cosmic data only with your explicit consent and using the strongest available protections. You may delete your cosmic profile at any time in Settings > Cosmic Profile.

5. 🤝 How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

With Other Users

Your public profile (photos, bio, zodiac sign, interests, location city) is visible to other users as part of how simmmy works. Your private messages are only visible to you and the recipient. Sensitive data (birth time, psychological scores) is shown only in aggregate compatibility percentages.

With Service Providers

We use carefully vetted third-party vendors to help operate simmmy, including cloud hosting, analytics, payment processing, customer support, and fraud detection. All vendors sign Data Processing Agreements and are bound to process data only on our instructions.

For Safety & Legal Reasons

We may share data with law enforcement, courts, or regulatory authorities when we are legally required to do so, or when necessary to protect the safety of our users or prevent serious harm.

Business Transfers

If simmmy is acquired, merged, or undergoes a restructuring, your data may be transferred to the relevant parties as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

We never share your data with advertisers for targeted advertising purposes without your explicit opt-in consent.

6. ⏱️ How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: For as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services
  • Deleted accounts: Core profile data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Safety retention: Certain data (e.g., blocked/banned user records) retained for up to 2 years to protect community safety
  • Transaction records: Kept for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting laws
  • Support records: Kept for 3 years from last contact
  • Legal holds: Data subject to legal proceedings may be retained until resolution
  • Cosmic profile data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion, unless you have active matches

After retention periods expire, data is either permanently deleted or irreversibly anonymised.

9. 🔐 Security

Protecting your data is something we take extremely seriously. We implement:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • End-to-end encryption for private messages
  • Multi-factor authentication options for your account
  • Regular independent security audits and penetration testing
  • 24/7 AI-powered threat monitoring
  • Strict access controls — only essential staff can access personal data
  • Photo verification and profile moderation to prevent fake accounts

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@simmmy.com.

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and relevant authorities within the legally required timeframes (72 hours under GDPR).

10. 👶 Children's Privacy

simmmy is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete all associated data.

If you believe a minor has created an account on simmmy, please report it immediately to safety@simmmy.com.

11. 🌍 International Data Transfers

simmmy operates globally, which means your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. When we transfer personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to countries without an adequacy decision, we use:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs) where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers

You may request a copy of the relevant safeguards by contacting us at privacy@simmmy.com.
