💳 Subscriptions & Billing
iOS: Go to your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Simmmy → Cancel.
Android: Open Google Play → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Simmmy → Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep Premium access until then.
EU/EEA/UK users are entitled to a full refund within 14 days of first subscribing. Certain US state residents have a 3-business-day cancellation right. For iOS refunds, contact Apple at getsupport.apple.com. For Google Play, request via Google Play. For all other billing queries, email billing@simmmy.com.
