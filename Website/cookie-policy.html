1. 🍪 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an app. They allow the service to remember information about your visit, making your experience smoother, more personalised, and more secure.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage / Session Storage: Browser-side storage for app state (e.g., theme preference, draft messages)
  • Pixel Tags / Web Beacons: Tiny invisible images used to track whether emails are opened or pages are visited
  • SDKs: Software libraries in our mobile app that collect usage and performance data
  • Device Fingerprinting: Used only for fraud prevention and security purposes

Throughout this policy, "cookies" refers to all of these technologies collectively.

2. 🎯 Why We Use Cookies

simmmy uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To keep you logged in to your account securely
  • To remember your theme (light/dark) and language preferences
  • To protect against fraud, bots, and account hijacking
  • To understand how users interact with the app so we can improve it
  • To measure the performance of our features and matching algorithms
  • To deliver personalised match suggestions and daily cosmic briefings

We do not use cookies to build advertising profiles to sell to third parties, nor do we allow advertising networks to place tracking cookies on simmmy without your explicit consent.

3. 📦 Types of Cookies We Use

🔒

Essential Cookies Always Active

These are strictly necessary for simmmy to function. Without them, you cannot log in, stay authenticated, or use core features. These cannot be disabled. They include session tokens, CSRF protection tokens, and security identifiers.

⚙️

Functionality Cookies Optional

These remember your preferences to personalise your experience — such as your theme choice (dark/light), notification settings, language preference, and whether you've seen certain onboarding content.

📊

Analytics Cookies Optional

These help us understand how users navigate simmmy, which features are most used, where users encounter difficulties, and how we can improve the app. Data is aggregated and anonymised. We use a privacy-first analytics provider. No personally identifiable information is stored.

🛡️

Security & Fraud Prevention Always Active

Used to detect and prevent fraud, fake accounts, and automated abuse. These are essential for maintaining the safety and integrity of our platform and cannot be disabled.

📣

Marketing Cookies Optional — Consent Required

Used only with your explicit consent to measure the effectiveness of our own promotional campaigns (e.g., understanding how many users who saw an ad installed simmmy). We do not use third-party advertising tracking cookies within the app itself.

5. ⚙️ Your Cookie Choices

You are in control of your cookie preferences. Here are your options:

  • Cookie Banner: When you first visit simmmy, you'll be presented with a cookie banner. Choose "Accept All," "Necessary Only," or customise your preferences.
  • In-App Settings: Visit Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences at any time to update your choices.
  • Browser Settings: You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent simmmy from working correctly.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw consent for optional cookies at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of any processing that occurred before withdrawal. When you withdraw consent for analytics or marketing cookies, we will stop placing those cookies immediately.
